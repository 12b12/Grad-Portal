About the Page

Welcome to the School District’s Graduates & Alumni Portal! 🎓This site was created to celebrate the achievements of our students and to keep our community connected across generations.

Here, families, friends, and future graduates can explore information about each graduating class by year and school. Discover details such as a student’s name, class rank, academic honors, chosen college, intended major, and a personal “About Me” section that shares their story.

We also highlight the journeys of our alumni, showing where they are now and how they continue to make us proud. This platform is more than a record of graduates—it’s a space for inspiration, celebration, and connection across our district.
